# Teacher Pipeline contract

Version: `teacher-pipeline-v1`

Phase 0 implements schema, validation, provenance, consent, retention, deletion, export eligibility, and review-state contracts only. Records live in a training workspace boundary and do not share the formal work database. Private content defaults to non-exportable and cannot enter a shared dataset without explicit consent and review.

No ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, or other real external teacher was called. No training dataset was produced from user novels.
